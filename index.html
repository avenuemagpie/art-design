Places in the Bay Area that Drive the Concept of STEAM

I know my daughter is her own person. I can still dream of sharing with her a world of augmented realities, of internet of things and of useful smart devices. I want her to be aware of a world that makes use of a part of her mind that can appreciate a particular aesthetic, can map spatial dimensions in her mind, and emphasizes learning by doing. Making and shaping something with your hands can bring a special joy and growth of mind. To that end, I search for communities and activities that nurture her confidence. I look for structures that can give her a scaffold to tackle, yet appreciate, the beautiful, complex realities that make up life and living.

There are a couple places in the San Francisco Bay Area that I think helps to achieve this.

  • Children's Creativity Museum
  • CuriOdyssey
  • Randall Museum
  • Chabot Space and Science Center
  • Bay Area Discovery Museum
  • Tech Museum of Innovation
  • Lawrence Hall of Science
  • The Exploratorium
  • Local Library programs
  • At Home

What experiences do you think provides momentum into STEAM for you and your loved ones?
