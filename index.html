A Note from the Avenue Magpie

Projects under the Avenue Magpie interpret my experiences of design and culture while living in San Francisco.

I explore techniques and materials in my awareness to make my world more meaningful and beautiful. I find ways to involve my daughters growing mind with complementary, scaled down fun.

My life goal? Facilitate harmony and create joy, but mostly engage my capacities for empathy, creativity and curiosity to its fullest.

Thanks for reading.
