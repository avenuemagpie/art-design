Places in the Bay Area that Drive the Concept of STEAM

I know my daughter is her own person. I still aim to share with her what I know of augmented realities, of internet of things and of useful smart devices. She can see this world that will engage her mind to appreciate a particular aesthetic, will help map spatial dimensions to her mind, and emphasizes learning by doing. To make and to shape something with your hands brings a special joy and growth of mind. I keep an eye out for activities to nurture her confidence. I look for structures to give her a scaffold to tackle and appreciate beautiful, complex realities.

There are a couple places in the San Francisco Bay Area that I think helps to achieve this.

  • Children's Creativity Museum
  • CuriOdyssey
  • Randall Museum
  • Chabot Space and Science Center
  • Bay Area Discovery Museum
  • Tech Museum of Innovation
  • Lawrence Hall of Science
  • The Exploratorium
  • Local Library programs
  • At Home

What experiences do you think provides momentum into STEAM for you and your loved ones?
